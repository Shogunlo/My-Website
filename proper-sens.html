Proper Sensitivity How To?

400 DPI Recommendations

When it comes to 400 DPI, the biggest downside is going to be using your mouse for regular desktop activities. In games, however this DPI is tried and true know for starting you off with a great spot for control. I'd recommend starting with a higher in game sensitivity as most games will let you go anywhere from a 1x multiplier all the way to 6x. This will look like .5-.55 depending on the game. This will set you up right about 200-250 eDPI

800 DPI Recommendations

800 DPI is both a popular choice and my personal recommendation for those just getting started. It offers a great balance between desktop use and actual in game use. At 800 DPI you find a good sensistivity with just about any game without having to change any .ini setting multipliers. Assuming you are playing a game with a 1x multiplier you would want you sensitivity right around .25-.35.
