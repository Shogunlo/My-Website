Proper Sensitivity How To?

400 DPI Recommendation

When it comes to 400 DPI, the biggest downside is going to be using your mouse for regular desktop activities. In games, however, this DPI is tried and true, known for starting you off with a great spot for control. I'd recommend starting with a higher in-game sensitivity, as most games will let you go anywhere from a 1x multiplier all the way to 6x.

800 DPI Recommendation

800 DPI is both a popular choice and my personal recommendation for those just getting started. It offers a great balance between desktop use and actual in-game use. At 800 DPI you find a good sensitivity with just about any game without having to change any ini setting multipliers.

1600 DPI Recommendation

1600 DPI is my personal choice, as it often helps your mouse meet its higher reporting rates. While I use it, I wouldn't recommend it for most people, as a lot of games, especially single-player games, have issues allowing you to lower your sensitivity enough. I recommend 1600 DPI for anyone whose mouse has a 2000 Hz-4000 Hz polling rate, as it can help you get the most out of your mouse.

eDPI: Effective Mouse DPI is your actual sensitivity in a game, calculated by multiplying your mouse DPI by your in-game sensitivity.
